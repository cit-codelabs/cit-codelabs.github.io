This is a step-by-step guide that will allow you to create a Billing Account on the Google Cloud Platform.

Before you begin you should have your 16-character coupon code provided by your instructor. The code will be in the following format:

XXXX-YYYY-ZZZZ-WWWW

Important:

When redeeming your code, the credit will be applied to whichever Google account you are currently logged in with.

To avoid any accidents, it's best to open an Incognito/private window in your browser before proceeding with these instructions.

https://console.cloud.google.com/education

You should log in with the Google account you will use throughout the semester. You can use a university email address that is associated with a Google account (if your school uses Gmail, for example) or a personal Google account.

Pro Tip:

Most instructors prefer that students use their university-sponsored email address if possible. This makes collaboration on projects easier.

When asked, paste or type the 16 character coupon code that was provided by your instructor into the Coupon code field.

The page should show your credit amount, expiration date, and the title of the course as entered by your instructor.

Choose "Accept and continue"

Note the name of your billing account. The default billing account name matches the original name of the course as provided by the instructor. In this example, the billing account name is "Information Technology Fundamentals."

You can rename the billing account to something more meaningful to you by choosing the Manage link and editing the name.

You will also see an overview of the promotional credits provided to you. By default, student coupons give $50.00 of credit, but this amount may vary depending on the needs of the course.

Once you see the promotional credit applied to the billing account, you have completed this process. You should next create a new Google Cloud project and choose the appropriate billing account.

One billing account can support approximately 10 Google Cloud projects.
